knob-fitting.scad, oven-fitting-bottom.scad and oven-fitting-top.scad are files that must be rendered and printed with OpenSCAD. 
oven-fitting.scad is the two sides of oven-fitting-bottom.scad and oven-fitting-top.scad, the geometry is more complex to print as one piece and requires high amounts of bridging. 
variables.scad contains a list of variables used by all .scad files.
